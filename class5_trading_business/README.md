# Class 5: Trading Business & Advanced Topics

**Duration**: 4 weeks (20 lessons)
**Time per lesson**: 45 minutes (30 min lecture + 15 min hands-on)
**Prerequisites**: Classes 1-4 (Fundamentals through Taxes)

---

## Overview

Class 5 covers professional-level trading structures and advanced concepts for serious traders. These strategies can save $30,000-$80,000+ annually for qualifying traders.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Understand Trader Tax Status (TTS) qualification
- Structure trading as a business (LLC, S-Corp)
- Apply Mark-to-Market accounting (Section 475)
- Navigate compliance and explore automation
