# Class 4: Taxes & Portfolio Maintenance

**Duration**: 4 weeks (20 lessons)
**Time per lesson**: 45 minutes (30 min lecture + 15 min hands-on)
**Prerequisites**: Classes 1-3 (Fundamentals, Technical Analysis, Strategies)

---

## Overview

Class 4 covers critical knowledge for preserving and growing wealth through tax efficiency and proper portfolio management. Understanding taxes can save you thousands of dollars annually.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Understand capital gains taxation and optimization
- Master tax-advantaged accounts (IRA, 401k, HSA)
- Apply tax-loss harvesting and asset location strategies
- Build sustainable portfolio management practices
